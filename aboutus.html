Welcome to Oceanuity

The Oceanuity Organizaiton was founded in 2035 by our founder Lee H. Daniel. He was a young marine biologist that yearned for a place where people of all ages and backgrounds could gather and explore the vast life that is the ocean. We have partnered with thousands of scientists and marine biologists across the globe alike to accumulate and acquire all our knowledge. We want knowledge to be shared to everyone who seeks for it. And why not better than to have it all in one place?
