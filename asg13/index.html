The Ideal Job

I have always had an interest in photography/videography. This does cover a very wide range of photography/videography as a whole but I enjoy taking pictures of people/food/animals. I also like recording videos of moments from people to landscapes to everyday objects. I feel that jobs that let me explore my abilities in this manner would be the most beneficial for me. Especially the jobs where creative freedom is quite large. I can imagine myself in freelance photography, but I can see myself promoting companies like products or real estate. The ideal job would be one where I am free to express my outlook on photography or allowing me to promote a group of people/business while still having enough framework that it can actually pay me something.
